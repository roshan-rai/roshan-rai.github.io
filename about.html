About me

Hello and Namaste

I'm Roshan Rai, originally from Lalitpur, Nepal. My educational journey began at Nalanda Secondary Boarding School in Nepal, where I spent my formative years until the 10th grade. Seeking a broader experience, I ventured to UWC Costa Rica for high school, earning my IB diploma. The next chapter led me to the University of Oklahoma, initially aiming for a mechanical engineering degree. However, the evolving landscape of technology drew me towards Management Information Systems (MIS). Life has been a thrilling roller coaster, fulfilling my dream of studying in the US. Costa Rica provided a diverse environment with people from nearly 70 different countries living in our boarding school. This experience broadened my horizons and taught me invaluable life lessons. One of my passions is traveling; I find joy in exploring new places. From Nepal to Costa Rica and now the USA, each journey adds a unique flavor to my story. I completed a study abroad program in South Korea in Fall 2022, embracing a different learning environment. Beyond academics, I play basketball, sing, and play guitar for fun. I love learning new skills, especially new physical activities. The future holds exciting possibilities, and I can't wait to unfold what lies ahead.
